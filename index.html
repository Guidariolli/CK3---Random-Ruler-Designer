About the CK3 Random Ruler Designer

This is a free, unofficial Crusader Kings III Ruler Designer generator. Instead of building a character by hand, it rolls a full random ruler for you — six skills, a batch of personality, education and lifestyle traits, plus a random culture and faith — while keeping the total within the 400-point cap so the character stays achievement and ironman eligible.

The point math was reverse-engineered from the base game files and calibrated against real in-game totals: trait costs, skill costs (including how trait modifiers change what each skill point costs) and the age curve. Filter by the DLCs you own, pick a focus attribute to shape the build, set a minimum point budget, and lock culture, faith or age when you want to keep them fixed between rolls.

A personal note: I originally built this for myself and still use it in my own playthroughs — I just figured it was worth sharing with the community. It's a free hobby project, so I hope you find it useful too.

Frequently asked questions

Does it respect the 400-point achievement cap?

Yes. Builds stay under the 400-point cap by default so they remain achievement and ironman eligible, and every roll shows a full breakdown of age, skills and traits. You can also switch the cap off if you just want inspiration.

Which DLCs are supported?

Tick the DLCs you own and the generator only pulls traits you can actually use. Traits tied to Roads to Power, All Under Heaven, Wandering Nobles and Tours and Tournaments are mapped, and base-game-only players still get valid rulers.

What is a focus build?

Pick an attribute — Diplomacy, Martial, Stewardship, Intrigue, Learning or Prowess — and the generator biases the education, the top skill and supporting traits toward it. Leave it on “Random (no focus)” for a fully random ruler.

Is it free and official?

It is completely free, runs entirely in your browser with no login and no ads. It is an unofficial fan-made tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Paradox Interactive.

Was this built with AI?

Yes — the site was fully vibe-coded with AI (Claude). To be upfront: the generator itself is not AI, it's a plain randomizer that uses point costs reverse-engineered from Crusader Kings III's base game files. It's a free hobby project — nothing is paywalled, and the optional “Buy me a coffee” is entirely voluntary and only helps cover hosting and time.
